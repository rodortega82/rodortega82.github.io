About Me

Hi, I’m Rodrigo, a CGI artist with close to 20 years of experience in creating visual magic. Originally from Brazil, my journey has taken me across the globe—from six years in China, where I honed my craft, to now being based in London. Currently working as a CGI Director, I specialize in turning concepts into immersive visuals, blending realism with creativity.

With a background in Industrial Design, I’ve developed a versatile skill set that spans high-end CG images and dynamic 3D motion graphics. Over the years, I’ve had the chance to work with iconic brands like Adidas, Apple, Audi, and Cartier, pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in visual storytelling.
